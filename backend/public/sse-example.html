🔔 SSE Job Notifications Example

Real-time job status updates using Server-Sent Events

Events Log

How It Works

1. Click "Generate Flashcards" → Gets jobId
2. Click "Connect to SSE" → Opens EventSource connection
3. Receive real-time updates:
   - connected: Initial connection
   - active: Worker picked up job
   - completed: Job finished successfully
   - failed: Job failed
   - heartbeat: Every 30s to keep connection alive
4. Click "Disconnect" to close connection
